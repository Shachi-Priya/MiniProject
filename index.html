Mini Project

(by shachi)

Mini Project is made up of 4 projects - The Calc App, The Clock App, The Note App and The weather App.

All the projects in this Mini Project was built in HTML, CSS and Javascript

Project1 : The Calc App

Project2 - The Clock App

The clock which shows current running time through analog clock on the click of start button and stops whenever the stop button clicked.

Project3 - The Note App

The Note App in which we can write our notes with their titles and that will save according to stack property(LIFO) in local storage.

Project4 - The Weather App

The Weather App which shows current weather, some upcoming weather of the day and weather of next 4 days of any city in the World which is fetched from Open Weather Map API.
